~/archlinux

Instructions

1. Verify my GPG signatures by installing the package mikelpint-keyringAUR.

2. Copy the following text at the bottom of /etc/pacman.conf:

[mikelpint]
SigLevel = Required
Server = https://mikelpint.github.io/repository/archlinux

3. Update your system with sudo pacman -Syu.
